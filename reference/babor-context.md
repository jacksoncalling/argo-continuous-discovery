# Babor Beauty Group — Reference Context

## Company Profile

**BABOR BEAUTY GROUP** — a family-owned German cosmetics company and global player,
headquartered in Aachen. Produces at the 60,000m² Beauty Cluster. Operates in 70+ countries.

**Two business arms:**
1. **BABOR Brand** — own retail stores, e-commerce (babor.com with AI Hautanalyse,
   Produktberater, BABOR & me loyalty program with b.points), beauty spas, professional
   partner network (institutes using Derma Visualizer and Beauty Pass)
2. **Private Label / Full-Service** — product development, manufacturing, consulting,
   marketing for clients including dermatologists, hotels, influencers, and startups

## The PM Persona

A Product Manager responsible for:
- Product concept development with international clients and internal R&D
- New product launches and relaunches — ownership from briefing to launch
- Strategic direction of national and international assortments
- Cross-functional project management: clients, key account, procurement, production, R&D
- Product costing in coordination with key account management

## Discovery Context

She interviews across the vertically integrated value chain:

| Segment | Examples | What They Care About |
|---------|---------|---------------------|
| **End consumers** | Online shoppers, gift buyers, loyalty members | Product discovery, purchase ease, personalization |
| **Beauty professionals** | Spa managers (Babor-owned), independent estheticians | Professional tools, client retention, product knowledge |
| **Internal teams** | R&D, production, sales | Feasibility, capacity, market positioning |
| **B2B clients** | Hotels, dermatology practices, startups | Private Label capabilities, speed, customization |

## Channel Complexity

Babor's channels sometimes compete with each other:

| Channel | Owner | Tension |
|---------|-------|---------|
| Own retail stores | Babor | Competes with online for foot traffic |
| Online shop (babor.com) | Babor | Competes with professional partners for product sales |
| Professional partner network | Independent operators | Lose sales when clients buy online after in-institute consultation |
| Private Label | External clients | May compete with Babor's own brand in some markets |

**The operator must recognize when an "opportunity" is actually a channel conflict
requiring escalation rather than product-level triage.**

## Key Babor Digital Tools

- **AI Hautanalyse** — online skin analysis tool on babor.com
- **Produktberater** — product recommendation quiz (asks for Hauttyp, Bedürfnisse)
- **Derma Visualizer** — professional in-spa skin analysis camera (80x magnification)
- **Beauty Pass** — in-spa client profile storing treatment history and analysis results
- **BABOR & me** — loyalty program with b.points for online purchases
- **Professional Partner Portal** — ordering and client management for certified partners
