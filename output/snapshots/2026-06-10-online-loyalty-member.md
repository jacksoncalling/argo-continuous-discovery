## Coaching Note

**Interview quality: Thin.** The opening question ("What are your thoughts on...?") invited opinion rather than story. When the interviewee offered a feature request (subscription box), the follow-up confirmed the request instead of probing the experience behind it. Torres recommends: "Tell me about the last time you [did X]" to ground the interviewee in a specific, real event. Abstract questions produce abstract answers — they drift from reality.
